Level 1

Every character at level one starts out with 2 Themes (one must not be a Combat Style), 1 Feat, 14 Background Points, Proficiency in 1 Talent or Craft Skill, Proficiency in 1 native language, and 3 Knowledge Skills.

Background Bonuses

Every character starts out with 14 background points and may spend them in many different ways. The chart below shows some ways to spend your Background Points but your DM may approve other ways of spending Background Points.

Stat Calculations

Recovery Value: Enhanced Ability Modifier of every Ability except Dexterity

Number of Recovery Surges: 4 + Constitution Modifier

Aura Strength: 20 + (Recovery Value * (1 + 1/3 Level))

Stamina: 10 + Enhanced Constitution Modifier + 1/3 level (Minimum of 7)

Skill Ranks: Intelligence Score + level * (4 + Intelligence Modifier)

You gain proficiency in a Knowledge skill for every 3 Enhanced Intelligence Modifier points.

You gain +1 to every Skill for every 3 Enhanced Wisdom Modifier points.

Size Categories

Creatures are divided up into Size Categories which give a general idea of the capabilities that a typical creature that size possesses. The chart below shows how a player character’s size affects Jump Reach, Jump Distance, and Inherent EC. The values in the size chart apply to a creature only if the creature does not already have those values specified.

Size Chart
Size Typical Height (cm) Inherent EC Jump Reach (m) Jump Distance (m) Approximate Move Speed (m)
Huge 360-480 -3 5 5 12
Large 240-360 -1 4 4 8
Medium 120-240 0 3 3 6
Small 60-120 1 2 2 5
Tiny 30-60 3 1 1 3
Note:Your actual Movement Speed is determined by your race.
Note: Jump Reach is how high up one can reach while jumping (measured from the ground). Note: Jump Distance is how far one can jump without a running start.
