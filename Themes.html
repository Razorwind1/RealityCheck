At level 1 you choose 2 Themes, but one of them must not be a Combat Style. You also gain a Theme at Levels 5, 10, 15, and 20.

Combat Styles, Practices, and Devine Pledges are all Themes.

Combat Styles

Unarmored and Unarmed

  • +6 Stamina
  • Instinctive Fighting [feat]: You may add your Wisdom modifier to EC when unarmored
  • You may spend 1 Energy to give your oncoming Unarmed Attack +1 AV. (can do this multiple times per attack)
  • Exposing Strike [feat]: When you deal damage to a target with an Unarmed Attack, you may spend 2 Energy to expose the target.
  • You may use one more Stamina each round as Energy.

Graceful Monk

  • +5 Stamina
  • If you are not wearing armor, your unarmed strikes have +1 AV and you gain +1 EC.
  • You gain +1m to your Movement Speed.
  • You ignore the penalty for turning more than 45 degrees during movement if not wearing Medium or Heavy armor.
  • You are treated as one size larger for the purposes of calculating jump height and distance.
  • You may spend 1 Energy to avoid all Opportunity Attacks for the rest of the round.
  • You may use one more Stamina each round as Energy.

Rapid Unarmed Striker

  • +5 Stamina
  • Once during a round, you may use a Swift Action to make an Unarmed Attack. This may only be done up to a number of times equal to your Enhanced Dexterity Modifier per encounter.
  • Your third unarmed strike each round against the same target has +5 AV.

Precise Ki Strikes

  • +3 Stamina
  • Predict their moves: You may spend a Standard Action to focus on a target. Until you are hit by the target, your Unarmed Attacks have Precision equal to your Wisdom modifier against the target. You may only focus on one target at a time.
  • Whenever your Unarmed Attack is a critical hit, you stun the target for the rest of the round.
  • Draining Strikes [feat]: When you deal damage to a target with an Unarmed Attack, you may spend 1 Energy. If you do so, you the target loses 1 Stamina. If you use Draining Strikes on a target 3 times during an encounter, they lose an additional 3 Stamina.

Fists of Wrath

  • +3 Stamina
  • If you are unarmed you may spend 1 Energy to have your oncoming combat maneuver impose Disadvantage on the Fortitude Save of the target.
  • You may spend a Swift Action to gain Advantage on the Attack Roll of all Unarmed Attacks you make this round and add your Strength Modifier to the damage of such attacks.
  • When you damage a target with an Unarmed Attack, you gain +5 AV to any further attack against them this round.

Meditative Monk

  • +5 Stamina
  • Instinctive Fighting [feat]: You may add your Wisdom Modifier to EC when unarmored.
  • Quick Recovery [feat]: Using a Standard Action to regain Energy regains 2 Energy instead of 1.
  • When making an Unarmed Attack, you may spend 1 Energy to impose Permanent Disadvantage on your targetâ€™s EC roll, but you provoke Opportunity Attacks.
  • You may use one more Stamina each round as Energy.
