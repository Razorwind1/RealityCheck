Feats have 5 tiers:

  • 0 = Background
  • 1 = Levels 1-4
  • 2 = Levels 5-9
  • 3 = Levels 10-14
  • 4 = Levels 15-20

Background Feats may be taken using Background Points during character creation. At each Tier, all previous feats are also available.

For example, at level 9, you have access to Background Feats, Tier 1 Feats, and Tier 2 Feats.

At each level, a single Feat may be swapped out for another of its tier or lower (one must still maintain the minimum number of non-combat feats based on their Level).

You cannot gain the same feat twice! (unless otherwise specified)

Every third Level, you must have an additional Non-Combat Feat. This is intended to balance your character and give more flavor than having all purely combat-based abilities.

Non-Combat Feats

Social Adept Tier 0NC
When trying to blend into a crowd or be inconspicuous in a social setting, you may add your Charisma Modifier to any Stealth and Deceive Checks you make
Craftsman Tier: 0NC
You gain Proficiency in a Craft Skill and Knowledge.
More Than a Hobby Tier: 0NC
Gain +3 to a single craft skill you have.
Medical Assistant Tier: 1NC
You have Proficiency in Knowledge(Medical Supplies). You are gain Proficiency in Heal, and gain an extra +2 to any Heal checks made with medical supplies.
Intimidating Persona Tier: 1NC
You gain +4 to Intimidate and Discourage, and -4 to Reason and Inspire.
Intelligent Healer Tier: 0NC
You gain Proficiency in Heal. You may use your Intelligence Modifier for Heal Checks instead of your Wisdom Modifier.
Natural Knowledge Tier: 0NC
You may use your Wisdom Modifier instead of Intelligence Modifier for any Knowledge Proficiencies you acquired from hands-on experience and not from books or second-hand knowledge.
Feared Tier: 0NC
You have a nefarious reputation or an intimidating aura (you decide). Pretty much everywhere you go, people are frightened of you. This means you are more likely to get away with small crimes as most people are afraid to report you to the authorities, but could also have some downsides…
Game Strategist Tier: 0NC
You are very skilled at playing strategy games such as chess, hearts, or liar’s dice. Whenever making Checks to determine the outcome of a strategy game, you have Advantage.
Contortionist Tier: 0NC
You are very flexible and can contort your body in different ways. You have Advantage on Dexterity based Checks made to escape constraints (manacles, strait jacket, etc) and can fit into spaces twice as small as a normal person could.
Specialist Tier: 0NC
You are gain Proficiency twice in 1 Knowledge Skill.
Innocent Face Tier: 1NC
You have Advantage on Checks made to convince someone of your innocence or good intentions.
Art of Intimidation Tier: 0NC
You have Advantage on Intimidate Checks when not in combat.
Swimmer Tier: 0NC
You have Proficiency in Swim and your base Swim Speed is 5m when not wearing armor. [normally you have a base swim speed of 3m if Proficient in Swim]
Agile Runner Tier: 0NC
When you take a Movement Action, you suffer no movement penalty for changing direction by more than 45 degrees. You also always move an extra 1m when Sprinting. [Courier Power]
Intelligent Disabler Tier: 0NC
You may use your Intelligence Modifier instead of Dexterity with your Disable Device Skill. You also gain Proficiency in Disable Device.
